Study and relax at Dauch


Members of PASE have access to the Dauch Alumni Center during finals week as another place to study. The building will be open Monday–Friday, from 8:00 a.m.–11:30 p.m. There will be coffee and hot chocolate provided by Starbucks, games to take breaks with, drawings to enter for prizes, and snacks to re-energize.


Special Bonus on Tuesday

Relax on Tuesday from 1:00-3:00 p.m. with puppies to pet and free massages. Must show your PASE card or wear your PASE gear to qualify for a massage.
